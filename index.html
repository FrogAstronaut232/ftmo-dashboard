$50K FTMO Free Trial · G10 only. Live forward test of the G10 10-pair FX swing strategy on a $50,000 FTMO Free Trial account (FTMO-Demo server), running autonomously through MetaTrader 5, currently in Phase 1 (evaluation) sizing. Fires once per weekday at 08:05 AEST (= 22:05 UTC) on the daily close. Note: running G2 and G10 together on one account — cointegrating them into a single combined book — proved problematic in earlier testing, so for now we are testing the G10 strategy on its own. This free trial mirrors a paid FTMO evaluation as closely as possible (same rules, spreads, fills and swap rates) and is the last dry run before buying a real evaluation. The other tab shows the earlier old-broker dry run.
